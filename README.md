# Education ROI Project

## Overview

The Education ROI Project is an independent economics research initiative exploring whether university education remains one of the best investments available to young people in the 21st century.

The project examines the costs and benefits of higher education across different countries and compares university pathways with alternative forms of education and training.

## Research Question

Are universities still the best investment for young people in the 21st century?

## Objectives

* Compare tuition costs across countries.
* Analyse student living expenses.
* Examine graduate earnings.
* Estimate the return on investment (ROI) of higher education.
* Explore alternative educational pathways.
* Evaluate the economic benefits of higher education for society.

## Countries Included

* United Kingdom
* United States
* Netherlands
* Germany
* Russia
